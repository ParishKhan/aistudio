[Claude Sonnet 5](/claude-sonnet-5) launched June 30 as Anthropic's new
default model for Free and Pro plans on claude.ai — and with an unusual
pricing move: introductory API rates of $2 per million input tokens and $10
per million output through August 31, 2026, rising to the standard $3/$15 on
September 1.

During the intro window, that undercuts [GPT-5.6 Terra](/gpt-5-6-terra)
($2.50/$15) in the mid-tier bracket while matching the 1M-token context
window of Anthropic's bigger models. Teams planning around the mid-tier
should note the September step-up when budgeting —
[the Terra comparison](/gpt-5-6-terra-vs-claude-sonnet-5) prices both sides.
